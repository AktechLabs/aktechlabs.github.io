Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Tmini ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our SSH terminal application for iOS.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Tmini is designed with privacy as a core principle.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • SSH Connection Details: Server hostnames, usernames, port numbers, and connection preferences you configure
  • SSH Keys: Private and public SSH keys you import or generate within the app
  • App Settings: Your preferences for font size, colour scheme, and other personalisation options
  • Terminal History: Command history within terminal sessions

How We Protect Your Data

  • Local Storage Only: All your data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers
  • iOS Keychain: SSH keys are securely stored in the iOS Keychain with device-level encryption
  • Biometric Protection: App access can be protected with Face ID or Touch ID
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your data to any cloud services

Third-Party Services

Tmini does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates independently without external connections except for your direct SSH connections to your servers.

SSH Connections

When you connect to SSH servers using Tmini:

  • Connections are made directly between your device and your servers
  • We do not intercept, monitor, or store any data from your SSH sessions
  • All communication is encrypted using standard SSH protocols
  • Connection logs and terminal output remain only on your device

Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. Your SSH connections, keys, and terminal sessions are private and remain exclusively on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed as a professional development tool.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@aktechlabs.com

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete from our systems. You maintain full control over all app data stored locally on your device and can delete it at any time by removing the app.
